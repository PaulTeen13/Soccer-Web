Leagues

There are many leagues in soccer. However, the top 5 leagues are all in Europe. They are the Premier League, Bundesliga, LaLiga, Serie A, and Ligue 1. The Premier League is in England and it has 20 football clubs in it. Since the Premier League has been created it has been 30 years. The Bundesliga is in Germany and it was found in 1962 and started playing in 1963. LaLiga was found in 1929 in Spain. So this leagues is very old and it has been almost 100 years. The Seria A is an Italian soccer league and it was founded in 1929 just like the LaLiga. The Ligue 1 is in France and it is 92 years old founded in 1932. However, there is another league in Europe than the other 5 leagues I have introduced. It is called in many ways UCL, Champions Leauge, or the Champs. The UCL was found in 1955 which means that this league is actually very old. The first to fourth place in each league qualifies into the UCL and the 5th place qualify into UEL the UEFA Europa league.
